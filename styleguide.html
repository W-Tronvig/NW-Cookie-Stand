Our History

Pat and Pattie's Salmon Cookies was founded in 1984 by Pat Murphy and his wife Pattie. Pat and Pattie both studied marine biology at the University of Washington and had their affinity for cooking and baking combined with their intellectual backgrounds merge together in a unique blend of two amazing foods.
When they first met, Pat decided to cook a salmon dinner for their first date and Pattie had brought over her world famous peanut butter cookies. After an amazing dinner and dessert, Pat and Pattie wondered if they could combine this great meal into a quick, easy and affordable convenient food for all to enjoy and viola! Salmon Cookies was born!
Ever since the inception of Salmon Cookies, it has been a Seattle staple 34 years in the making. With twelve locations total down the West coast -- three of which are in Seattle Washington -- Pat and Pattie's dream has truly come to fruition.
